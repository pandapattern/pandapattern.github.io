**Panda Pattern** was launched in 2025 as a PDF digital pattern cutting assistant tool. Through it, sewing enthusiasts can DIY projector pattern cutting, using their own projectors to project their PDF patterns onto fabric, achieving precise, paperless cutting to simplify the sewing process.

Our goal is simple: to help sewing enthusiasts DIY projector patterns in an open way, simplifying the sewing cutting process.

Enjoy handcrafting, simple creativity.
