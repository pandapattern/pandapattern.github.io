Panda Pattern focuses on DIY projector-based digital pattern solutions. We provide the Panda Pattern App, along with knowledge and tutorials on using projectors to cut fabric. We help sewing enthusiasts DIY projector patterns, using their own projectors to project their PDF patterns onto fabric for easy cutting.

### Advantages
Compared to traditional paper patterns, projector patterns are more convenient, faster, and eco-friendly.

### Open
Panda Pattern remains open, and we:
- Support sewing enthusiasts in DIY projector pattern solutions using their own projectors;
- Support sewing enthusiasts in using their own patterns in the Panda Pattern App;
- Support designers in adapting their designed patterns to work with the Panda Pattern App for a better user experience;
