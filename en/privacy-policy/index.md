# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: January 2025**

Thank you for using Panda Pattern ("we," "our," or "the App"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

## 1. Information Collection

### 1.1 Types of Information We Collect

When you use our application, we may collect the following types of information:

**Account Information:**
- Email address
- Password (encrypted storage)
- User ID

**Usage Information:**
- Application usage data
- Device information (device model, operating system version, unique device identifier)
- IP address
- Access time and date
- Feature usage statistics

**Content Information:**
- PDF files you upload
- Pattern data you create or edit
- Preference settings you save

**Technical Information:**
- Log files
- Error reports
- Performance data

### 1.2 How We Collect Information

We collect information through:
- Information you directly provide (account registration, file uploads, etc.)
- Automatically collected information (usage analytics, device information, etc.)
- Third-party services (such as cloud storage services)

## 2. Information Use

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

**Service Provision:**
- Create and manage your account
- Process and store PDF files you upload
- Provide projector pattern cutting assistance features
- Provide customer support

**Service Improvement:**
- Analyze usage patterns to improve application features
- Fix errors and performance issues
- Develop new features

**Communication:**
- Send service-related notifications
- Respond to your inquiries and requests
- Send important updates and security notifications

**Legal Compliance:**
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- Protect our rights and property
- Prevent fraud and abuse

## 3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We only share your information in the following circumstances:

**Service Providers:**
- We may share information with third-party service providers to help us operate the service (such as cloud storage, analytics services)
- These service providers must comply with strict confidentiality obligations

**Legal Requirements:**
- We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

**Business Transfers:**
- Your information may be transferred in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale

**With Your Consent:**
- We may share information with your explicit consent

## 4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

- Use encryption technology to protect data transmission and storage
- Implement access controls and authentication
- Conduct regular security audits
- Restrict employee access to personal information

Despite these measures, please note that no data transmission or storage system is 100% secure.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your information for the following periods:

- **Account Information:** Retained during your account's active period and for a period thereafter
- **Usage Data:** Retained according to legal requirements and business needs
- **Content Data:** Retained until you delete your account or explicitly request deletion

You can request deletion of your account and related data at any time.

## 6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you may have the following rights:

**Right of Access:**
- Request access to personal information we hold about you

**Right of Rectification:**
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete information

**Right of Erasure:**
- Request deletion of your personal information ("right to be forgotten")

**Right to Data Portability:**
- Receive your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format

**Right to Object:**
- Object to certain types of data processing

**Withdraw Consent:**
- Withdraw consent you previously gave

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided at the end of this policy.

## 7. Third-Party Services

Our application may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of these third parties.

We may use the following types of third-party services:
- Cloud storage services
- Analytics services
- Payment processing services

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that we have collected information from a child, we will immediately delete it.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to locations outside your country/region for processing and storage. We will ensure appropriate security measures are taken to protect your information.

## 10. Your Choices

- You can manage your privacy preferences in the application settings
- You can delete your account at any time

**Communication Preferences:**
- You can choose not to receive certain types of communications
